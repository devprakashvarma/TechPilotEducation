Start your computer journey here.

This comprehensive beginner's course is designed to take someone with zero computer knowledge to comfortable user status. A unique feature is our "Computer Anatomy" module that explains what each hardware component does in simple terms, helping users understand their machines better. The internet section covers safe browsing practices, creating strong passwords, and avoiding common online scams - crucial knowledge in today's digital world.

The course includes special troubleshooting modules for common problems like frozen applications, printer issues, and internet connectivity problems. Each lesson concludes with practical exercises that reinforce the skills learned. By course end, students can comfortably perform all basic computing tasks needed for daily life or entry-level office work.

Windows 11 Mastery

Unlock the power of your PC.

Our Windows 11 course goes far beyond the basics to explore the full potential of Microsoft's latest operating system. The curriculum begins with installation and setup guidance, then progresses through customization, productivity features, and advanced management tools. You'll learn how to personalize your Start menu and taskbar for maximum efficiency, use virtual desktops to organize workspaces, and master the new Snap Layouts for window management.

The course dedicates significant time to security features like Windows Hello, BitLocker encryption, and Microsoft Defender. We cover often-overlooked utilities like PowerToys, clipboard history, and the revamped Microsoft Store. Advanced modules explore registry editing (safely), Group Policy settings, and automation through PowerShell scripting basics.

A standout feature is our "Optimization" section that teaches how to speed up a sluggish PC, manage startup programs, and troubleshoot common performance issues. The course also compares Windows 11 with previous versions, highlighting key differences that affect daily use.

Practical projects include creating a customized development environment, setting up a home network, and configuring parental controls. By completion, students can navigate Windows 11 like power users, leveraging features most people never discover on their own.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are these courses really free?

Yes! All our YouTube tutorials and course materials are completely free to access. We believe in making computer education accessible to everyone regardless of their financial situation. There are no hidden charges or premium paywalls - what you see is what you get, and it's all free.

Do I need any prior computer knowledge?

Not at all! Our courses are designed for absolute beginners. We start from the very basics like "how to turn on a computer" and progress gradually. Even if you've never touched a computer before, our structured lessons will guide you step by step to computer literacy.

How are these courses different from others?

Our courses stand out in three key ways: 1) Bilingual instruction that makes technical concepts clear for Hindi speakers, 2) Practical focus with real-world examples rather than just theory, and 3) Dev Prakash's unique teaching style that breaks down complex topics into simple, digestible lessons.

Can I get a certificate after completing courses?

Currently, we don't offer formal certificates for our free YouTube courses as they are designed for skill-building rather than certification. However, we're working on a certification program for our premium courses (coming soon) that will include assessments and verifiable certificates.

How often is new content added?

We add new content every week! Our regular schedule includes: Tuesday Tips (short productivity videos), Friday Fundamentals (in-depth tutorials), and monthly "Ask Dev" sessions where we answer viewer questions. We also create special series based on trending topics and viewer requests.
